About Me

I’m an industrial reliability leader turning AI/ML engineer, building systems that keep machines, teams, and businesses performing at their best. I’ve led maintenance and automation through capital turnarounds, OEE improvement, and predictive maintenance—integrating PLCs, SCADA, robotics, and data-driven troubleshooting to cut downtime and lift throughput. Recently I overseen multimillion-dollar expansions while mentoring technicians and standardizing SOPs, spares strategy, and RCA/CAPA. To formalize the data side, I’m completing UT Austin’s Program in AI/ML with hands-on work in Python, EDA, supervised learning, ensembles, and deployment—so models don’t stay in notebooks; they ship and create real measurable impact. Portfolio projects include end-to-end pipelines (EDA → features → cross-validation → calibration) for visa/loan approval, churn, and reliability analyses. Fusing MLOps with operations—predictive maintenance, quality intelligence, and AI-augmented automation—owning data collection, modeling, deployment, monitoring, and continuous improvement.
